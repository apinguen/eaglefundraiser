The Project

Our project aims to enhance the science courtyard area at Fulton Middle School by installing benches with planter boxes. These benches, designed for durability and low maintenance, will offer students a comfortable space to relax, eat, study, and engage in various activities. By adding aesthetic appeal to the courtyard, we seek to transform it into a vibrant hub of student life, contrasting with its current minimal adornment of only two trees.

Furthermore, this project serves a dual purpose by expanding the courtyard into a popular student hangout spot while enhancing the overall attractiveness of the previously lackluster surroundings. By providing a designated area for students to gather and socialize, we aim to foster a sense of community and belonging within the school. Additionally, the addition of benches and planter boxes will contribute to the beautification of the campus, creating a positive and inspiring atmosphere for learning and recreation alike. With your support, we can transform this vision into reality and create a lasting impact on the students and faculty of Fulton Middle School.
